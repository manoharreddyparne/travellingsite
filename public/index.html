Introduction to the Application: Meet "Prayasmaya," a versatile travel companion that simplifies your journey by consolidating various functionalities into one user-friendly application. Whether you're online or offline, Prayasmaya is designed to address common challenges faced by travelers. Here's how it works: Map Assistance: Upon login and providing necessary details, users express their travel needs. Prayasmaya responds by assigning a personalized map sheet, tailoring your journey to your preferences. Trip Planning: Users complete a form detailing their trip requirements. The system, whether through a computer or a user interface, then assigns a map and a guide (which can be a robot or a consultant agency) to make your trip smoother. Budget Analysis: Prayasmaya features a "budget analyzer" to help users plan and understand the costs associated with their trips in advance. Local Support: The application collaborates with local guides, ensuring quick responses to urgent needs. Local guides provide instant support, making your trip more efficient and enjoyable. Consultancy Services: For specific points in your journey, Prayasmaya offers a "consultancy for a guide" option. Users can choose between a physical guide or virtual assistance, and the system assigns a suitable guide accordingly. On-the-Go Assistance: Prayasmaya senses and responds to user needs during travel, facilitating quick actions for any unforeseen challenges. If you find yourself stuck, the application promptly connects you with a local guide for immediate support. In essence, Prayasmaya aims to enhance your travel experience by streamlining the planning process, offering budget insights, and providing on-the-spot assistance through a combination of technology and local expertise.
